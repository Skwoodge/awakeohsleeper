# Awake Oh Sleeper Productions

Suicide awareness website for a punk/ska/reggae concert organization based in Prescott, AZ.

## Mission

"To unite people through the energy and authenticity of punk rock, ska, hardcore, and reggae music, using live concerts as a platform to spread hope, love, and connection. Raising awareness around mental health and suicide prevention, breaking stigma, and creating spaces where people feel seen, supported, and empowered to seek help."

## Active Effects (DO NOT break)
- Floating background particles (three.js via js/particles.js) — rendered on canvas
- Scroll progress bar at top of page (#scroll-progress)
- GSAP fade-in on hero text with 2s safety timeout
- GSAP scroll-triggered reveals on sections (.reveal, .reveal-left, .reveal-right, .stagger-children)
- Magnetic buttons (js/animations.js) — cursor-following pull on hover
- Button shimmer on hover (.btn-glow)
- Card tilt on hover (sponsor and band cards, js/animations.js)
- Animated section dividers between sections (.section-divider)
- Gradient "Louder" text via requestAnimationFrame (hero title highlight)
- Loading screen with animated "LOADING..." text
- Countdown timer to May 30, 2026 6:00 PM
- Mobile fixed "Get Help Now" button (bottom center, pulsing glow, hidden on desktop)

## Rejected / Removed Effects (DO NOT re-add)
- Three.js ember particles (replaced with original particles)
- Word-by-word text reveal (broke hero text)
- Scroll parallax (too subtle/laggy)
- Cursor glow circle (annoying)
- Glow guitar cursor (ridiculous)
- "Louder" neon flicker (seizure-inducing)
- "Louder" breathing glow (reverted)
