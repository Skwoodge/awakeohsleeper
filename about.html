Why We Exist

Uniting People Through
Raw Authenticity

"Awake Oh Sleeper Productions exists to unite people through the energy and authenticity of punk rock, ska, hardcore, and reggae music, using live concerts as a platform to spread hope, love, and connection."

We are committed to raising awareness around mental health and suicide prevention, breaking stigma, and creating spaces where people feel seen, supported, and empowered to seek help. Through music, community, and access to resources, we strive to remind every person that they are not alone and hope is always louder.

Based in Yavapai County, Arizona, our events bring together punk, ska, reggae, and hardcore communities — scenes built on solidarity, authenticity, and looking out for one another. In a world that often feels isolating, we create spaces where connection is inevitable.

We don't preach. We don't judge. We show up, turn up the amps, and remind everyone in the room that their life matters.

The Values That Drive Us

Community

Punk rock has always been about the misfits finding each other. We carry that tradition forward.

Hope

Darkness is real. But hope burns brighter. Every show, every conversation, every resource shared.

Action

Words without action are just noise. We connect people to real resources — 988, local support, community.

A Message From the Founders

Why We Do This

"Awake Oh Sleeper" comes from the Bible — a call to rise, to wake up, to be present. For us, it's a call to notice the people around you, to check in on your friends, to break the silence around suicide. We've lost people. We've struggled ourselves. And we've found healing in the pit, in the mosh pit, in the crowd singing along to songs that make you feel less alone.

This isn't a religious organization. This is a human one. Come for the music. Stay for the community. Leave knowing you matter.
