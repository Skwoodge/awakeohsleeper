You Are Not Alone

We See You.
We Hear You.
We Love You.

If you're reading this and struggling — please know that your life matters. Whatever you're going through right now, you don't have to go through it alone. This website, these shows, this community — it's all because someone out there needs to hear that they are loved.

We don't have all the answers. But we have each other. And that's a start. Reach out. Come to a show. Stand in a crowd of people who get it. Let the music remind you that you're still here for a reason.

Our Mission

Music Is the
Weapon Against Silence

"Awake Oh Sleeper Productions exists to unite people through the energy and authenticity of punk rock, ska, hardcore, and reggae music, using live concerts as a platform to spread hope, love, and connection."

We are committed to raising awareness around mental health and suicide prevention, breaking stigma, and creating spaces where you feel seen, supported, and empowered to seek help. Through music, community, and access to resources, we are here to remind you that you are not alone and hope is always louder than your struggle.

Community

You belong here. Punk rock has always been about the misfits finding each other — and that includes you.

Hope

Darkness is real. But hope is louder. Your story isn't over. Every show, every conversation matters.

Action

We connect you to real resources — 988, local support, and a community that gives a damn. You don't have to figure this out alone.
